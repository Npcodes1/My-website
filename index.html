Hello!

My name is Nicole Payne and I'm a coder! Come learn more about me and how/why this med tech is transitioning into tech!

I started coding back in 2022. I've always been interested in learning, but it wasn't until there was a Techlahoma event that I thought I'd give it a shot. I've been hooked ever since. As a molecular medical technologist, I come from a background in biology and healthcare. I've tested for local and national food outbreaks, bioterrorism exposures, Covid-19, HIV, HCV, and all kinds of STDs lol. With everything becoming automated in my field, the amount of hands-on work I do has become really limited. So I'm hoping to switch over to the other side to learn about software and web development. In my spare time, I like to destress with reading, tv, kickboxing, and yoga. I also like puzzles and brainteasers.
